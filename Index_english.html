THE WINE-GROWER

The estate will be certified Organic Agriculture in 2021.

Preserving and enhancing the terroirs, working in the most natural way possible, both in the vineyard and in our cellars, and promoting short circuits are our values.

We are part of an an organic farming approach to offer quality products at an accessible price to as many people as possible while respecting the sharing of added value.

OUR STORY

Our vineyards are cultivated by my family since my grandparents immigrated from Italy in 1933.

These were, in fact, long before us. Indeed, archaeological excavations have uncovered the remains of a Roman villa (the word villa meant an agricultural domain).

Many pieces of pottery and coins were found and identified by archaeologists. My grandfather’s greatest find, at a time when the work was done with the horses, was a small plate signed by his Roman potter who was called «Silvius».

This is how Domaine Villa Silvius is born!

THE ESTATE

It is located in the heart of the Rhone Valley. The vine varieties are constituted by the traditional type of vines of the Côtes du Rhône which are the Grenache and the Syrah but also of two new and rare red type of vines, integrated since recently in the specification of the appellation: the Couston and the Marselan

Today we wish to complete our commercialization mode by making our own bottles and thus build a relationship of trust with our customers

All the steps are carried out by ourselves at the estate: winemaking, bottling, labelling, and shipping.

The estate is ran exclusively by the family.
